Problem Statement 1


Many online fintech companies like https://www.lendingclub.com offer loans in four and five figures while charging interest rates that can range up to 25%, 30%, or more per year  at a time when the cost of funds to bankers remains at near-historic lows. Some fintech companies charge annual interest rates between 160% and 299%, in payday lender territory. The  fintech industry is operating at a scale that rivals the storefront payday lending industry (theintercept.com).
Online Lending Club, a peer to peer (P2P) fintech company has loan grades that categorizes borrowers based on their credibility.
My project intends to explore if Online Lending Club strategy facilitates secured Investment options and if the ROI is worth the risk Investors take in each grade of loan notes based on Net Charge Off Rate.
