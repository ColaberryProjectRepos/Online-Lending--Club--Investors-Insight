Conclusion and Suggestions 6
Suggestions:
Investors have come to trust the online lending club due to the high return on investment.

1) Due to high rate of net charge off for the borrowers in grade A, the online lending club administrators should take extra steps to identify the bankers of each borrowers aside from the compulsory background and credit checks. 

2) Intermittent inspection should also be conducted on the termed loans to ensure the credit customer still maintain healthy remittance habit and has not unduly overstretched his/her financial commitments to reduce the net charge off rate considerably.

3) Investors are encouraged to invest in loan notes in grade B that offers high return on investment and have very low default and Net charge off rate to ensure their investment are protected.
